What is Towdah?

Towdah is a Hebrew word for thanksgiving. It involves extending the hand in adoration, avowal, or acceptance.

Towdah '25 is a special time set aside for Celebrating the Goodness of God. It is a night of free worship where we gather to recount His blessings and faithfulness.
